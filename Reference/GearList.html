Gear List - Overnight

Sleep System

  • Sleeping Bag (0c,32f) with Compression Sack
      • Start with a cheap bag and try it out first it is likely you'll go through a couple before you find the style and sleep system you prefer.
    • Walmart Example 1
    • Walmart Example 2
    • Amazon Example 1
    • Amazon Example 2
      If you want to get expensive gear but arent certain what to start with then I recommend Millitary Issue or MilSpec Gear. This is gear thats been used with boot camp recruits for years Its the militaries idea of ultra light, cheap and durable. Granted its not the lightest the cheapest or the most durable but its a place to start.
    • Amazon Example #1
    • Amazon Example #2
    • Your Local Army Surplus store might have some actual Military Issue Sleeping Bags. Look over them carefully generally they will have a missing snap or broken zipper. The Military generally doesn't get rid of things in perfect condition.
  • Small Inflatable Pillow

Equipment

  • Backpack
      • Couple key things to keep in mind when shopping for a pack. The weight of your pack sits on your hips via a belt. The better the belt the more comfortable it will be. Bigger isnt necessarily Better. A 40L-45L Pack should be more than enough to carry all the gear you'll need for 4 seasons in Texas and 3 seasons in the Southern US. A larger pack tends to make you over pack and carry to much.
