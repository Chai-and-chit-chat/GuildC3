Awards

Tally Awards

    As an outdoor community or focus above all else is outdoor activities. For each of the following a pin is given.
  • Bronze, Silver and Gold Backpacking
    • Bronze - Given for each 5 miles of a backpacking trip where you pack in your gear stay at least one night and pack your gear back out.
    • Silver - Exchanged for 5 Bronze.
    • Gold - Exchanged for 4 Silver.
  • Bronze, Silver and Gold Camping
    • Bronze(Day) - Given for each night of camping.
    • Silver(Week) - Exchanged for 7 Bronze.
    • Gold(Month) - Exchanged for 4 Silver.
  • Bronze, Silver and Gold Canoeing
    • Bronze - Given for each 5 miles paddled (or 10 miles if in a two person craft).
    • Silver - Exchanged for 5 Bronze.
    • Gold - Exchanged for 4 Silver.
  • Bronze, Silver and Gold Hiking
    • Bronze - Given for each 5 miles of a day hike.
    • Silver - Exchanged for 5 Bronze.
    • Gold - Exchanged for 4 Silver.

Ranks

Though there are some minimum requirements for ranks they are not so much earned as awarded in accordance with the needs of the troop.
  1. Pradesh - Recriut
    • Memorize the Oath
    • Memorize the Law
    • Demonstrate how to Raise the flag of your country.
    • With the assistance of another fold the flag of your country.
  2. Pratham - Squad Leader\Guild Officer
    • Qualify as Civics
    • Qualify as Tools
    • Qualify as Fire Watch
    • Qualify as Ropework
    • Qualify as First Aid (Apprentice)
  3. Padhata - Guild Leader
    • Qualify as Aquatics (Apprentice)
    • Qualify as Hiking (Apprentice)
    • Qualify as Camping (Apprentice)
    • Qualify as Nutritionist (Apprentice)
    • Qualify in 1 skill of your choice for a total of 10 Qualifcations
    • 1 Silver Hiking
    • 2 Silver Camping
    • 1 Bronze Canoeing
  4. Ashwa - Troop Officer
    • Qualify as First Aid (Journeyman)
    • Qualify as 4 skill of your choice for a total of 15 Qualifcations
    • 2 Silver Hiking
    • 1 Gold Camping
    • 3 Bronze Backpacking
    • 3 Bronze Canoeing
  5. Ratha - Senior Guild Master
    • Qualify as Hiking (Journeyman)
    • Qualify as Camping (Journeyman)
    • Qualify as Nutritionist(Journeyman)
    • Qualify as 3 skills of your choice for a total of 20 Qualifcations
    • 1 Gold Hiking
    • 1 Gold Camping
    • 1 Silver Backpacking
    • 1 Silver Canoeing
  6. Gaja - Junior Troop Master
    • Qualify as BackPacking (Master)
    • Qualify as 2 other skills as a Master for a total of 25 Quals completed with 3 as a Master
    • 2 Gold Hiking
    • 2 Gold Camping
    • 2 Silver Backpacking
    • 2 Silver Canoeing
